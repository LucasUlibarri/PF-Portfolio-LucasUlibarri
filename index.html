Ruedas NG is a company in charge of the wholesale and distribution of alloy wheels, offering top quality products manufactured under rigorous quality standards. They were looking to refresh the style of their website with a cleaner and more modern look, so that the user can navigate and find their products in a simpler and clearer way. SimpliMuv arose as a solution for the different distributors to have a customised online shop according to the standards that the client demanded.
